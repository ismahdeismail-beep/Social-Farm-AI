# BENCHMARKING

## Purpose
Benchmarking allows users to compare their performance against competitors, industry standards, and internal historical data.

## Benchmarking Areas
- **Platform:** Compare performance across platforms.
- **Campaign:** Compare effectiveness of different campaigns.
- **Creator:** Compare engagement across different content creators.
- **Organization:** Compare workspace performance against organization-wide benchmarks.

## Implementation
Benchmarks are calculated by aggregating anonymized data from similar organizations, providing context to individual performance metrics.
