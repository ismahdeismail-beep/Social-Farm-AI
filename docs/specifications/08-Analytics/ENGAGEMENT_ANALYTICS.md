# ENGAGEMENT_ANALYTICS

## Purpose
Engagement Analytics captures the depth of interaction users have with content, which is a stronger signal than simple views.

## Key Metrics
- **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Impressions.
- **Sentiment:** AI-analyzed sentiment of comments (Positive, Neutral, Negative).
- **Interaction Breakdown:** Ratio of Likes vs. Comments vs. Shares vs. Bookmarks.
- **Conversion Rate:** CTR from content to external links or platform-specific goals.

## Significance
Engagement is the primary metric for training our AI's optimization agents to prefer high-interaction content.
